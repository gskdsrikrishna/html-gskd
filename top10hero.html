Superman

Superman is a fictional superhero created by writer Jerry Siegel and artist Joe Shuster. He first appeared in Action Comics #1 in 1938. Superman possesses superhuman strength, invulnerability, flight, and heat vision, among other abilities. He is known for his iconic costume featuring a blue suit with a red cape and the stylized "S" emblem on his chest.

Spider-Man

Spider-Man, created by writer Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko, made his first appearance in Amazing Fantasy #15 in 1962. Peter Parker, a high school student, gained superhuman abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider. Spider-Man is known for his agility, web-slinging abilities, and his iconic red and blue costume.
